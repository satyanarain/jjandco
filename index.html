  • Liaison with Government and RBI
  • Mergers & Acquisitions

BANK AUDIT

The division renders Stock Audit, Concurrent Audit, Information Security Audit and Statutory Branch Audit services to public sector banks:

  • Statutory Branch Audit of PNB

  • Stock Audit of OBC
  • Stock Audit of Bank of India
  • Information Security Audit of OBC

AUDIT & ACCOUNTS DIVISION

The division specializes in Audit of Government Audit and Accounting & Audit services of different types such as:

  • Due Diligence Audits

TAX & LAW DIVISION
